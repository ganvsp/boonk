What is Interior Designer?

  1. An interior designer is someone who works with a client to create aesthetic rooms and spaces.
  2. Clients range from homeowners to large corporations.
  3. The spaces and rooms interior designers create are equally varied, ranging from simple indoor and outdoor home environments to hotel lobbies and lavish mansions.
  4. No matter the size, every interior designer works to create spaces that are attractive yet functional. Spaces must also be safe while meeting the specific needs of the client.
  5. An interior designer is typically an independent contractor who must build a client base.
  6. In some instances, a designer will work for a larger firm. In either case, he or she must excel in order to build a positive reputation in the industry and secure future work.
